Our Story

VISION BTP SARL is an engineering and construction company based in Douala, Bonaberi. We specialize in building design, project execution, structural analysis, geotechnical studies, and sustainable construction solutions.

Our team combines technical expertise, modern engineering software (AutoCAD, Revit, ROBOT Structural Analysis), and practical field experience to deliver reliable, durable, and cost-effective structures.

We are committed to providing integrated engineering solutions from concept to completion while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and professionalism.
